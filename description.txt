Auto Piggy Bank automatically stores your coins into your piggy bank.

Client side configuration:
- Auto Piggy Bank Toggle (Default: On): toggles the mod's functionality for the current player
- Trigger On Money Trough interact (Default: Off): if enabled, the mod only puts the coins into the player's piggy once they use the Money Trough item

Server side configuration:
- Require Money Trough (Default: On): if enabled, the player has to have to have a Money Trough in their inventory for the mod to take effect
